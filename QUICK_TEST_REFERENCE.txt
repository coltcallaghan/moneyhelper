WHAT WAS FIXED
================================================================================

ISSUE: In Earliest FIRE and Target Age modes, ISA was not getting its full
       budget allocation. It was calculated as "remaining after SIPP" instead
       of having budget priority.

FIX:   Reordered allocation logic so ISA is built BEFORE SIPP in those modes.

FILES CHANGED:
  - src/actionPlanCivilian.js (lines 77-102)
  - src/actionPlanMOD.js (lines 121-146)
  - src/App.js (buildResults function and JSX)

EXAMPLE - MOD User, £40k contribution:
  Before fix (WRONG):  ISA ~£10k, SIPP ~£20k, AP ~£12k
  After fix (RIGHT):   ISA £20k, SIPP ~£8k, AP ~£12k
  ↑ ISA now gets full £20k limit
